About Us


The website's creator and primary researcher is Maxence Finestone, and it has been developed at the request of Karan Arya. Both Maxence and Karan are based in the United Kingdom, specifically at ACS Egham in London. This collaborative effort has resulted in the creation of this informative platform. The website was initiated by Karan Arya, who had a particular motivation for its development. Karan was planning a trip to Tokyo, Japan, but he lacked substantial knowledge about the city's attractions, activities, and places to visit. Recognizing the need for a resource that could assist travelers like himself, he enlisted Maxence's help in creating this platform. The primary goal of the website is to provide comprehensive information and insights for individuals traveling to Tokyo, who may be uncertain about what to do during their visit. It caters to a diverse audience, offering a wide range of activities and recommendations, including options for children, adults, cultural landmarks, as well as suggestions for bars and restaurants. The website aims to be a one-stop destination for travelers seeking valuable information on Tokyo's attractions and experiences, enhancing their trip planning and ensuring a memorable visit to this vibrant city.
